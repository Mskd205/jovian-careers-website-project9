The University's campus is located in the city of Kano State, kano, an ancient town in North-western Nigeria. Kano itself was excised from kano state on Tuesday August 27, 1991.

The state covers a total land area of about 22,410sq Km. It bordered on the west by Kano State, on the east Janguza and Gwarzo and on the North by Katsina State and Republic of Niger.
The University recognizes the importance of it's location in Kano and seeks to link it's research and teaching to the vast resources of these great metropolitan areas in the surrounding states.
